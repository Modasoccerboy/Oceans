Life Cycle/Reproduction

Animal life Cycle and Reproduction? One generation (the medusa) reproduces sexually and the next generation (the polyp) reproduces asexually. Moon jelly (Aurelia aurita) males in their medusa form, which can be seen in the Ring of Life exhibit, release sperm trails that are taken up orally by moon jelly females and fertilized internally.
