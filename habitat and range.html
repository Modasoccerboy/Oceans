Habitat and Range

Where does Moon Jellyfish live? Habitat. Moon jelly fish are generally seen close to shore, in harbor's and estuaries. They live in brackish waters with as salt content of 0.6% which is very low. Temperatures that favor their survival is 9 °C to 19 °C.
