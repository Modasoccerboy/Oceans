Species Survival Status

Moon Jellyfish is it species in danger of extinction?Moon jellies are not threatened or endangered but can serve as an indicator of marine ecosystems out of balance. Unlike other larger species (especially moon jelly predators), they can survive and thrive in otherwise uninhabitable waters.
